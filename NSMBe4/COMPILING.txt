Notes on Compiling NSMBe:

Prerequesits:
-Microsoft Visual Studio 2010
-.NET Framework 2.0 or higher

Compiling
-Open "NSMBe4.sln" in Visual Studio.
-Choose your compiling options*
-Under "Build", choose "Build Solution", or Ctrl+Shift+B

Locating your NSMBe Binary:
If your Compiling Options were: Release, Any CPU
-Your binary files will be located under NSMBe4/bin/Release
Reference README.txt for the necessary NSMBe files
